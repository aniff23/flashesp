## üîß How to Use FlashESP

1. **Install the Application**: Follow the steps in the "Download & Install" section.
2. **Launch the Application**: Locate the application in your applications folder or menu and open it.
3. **Connect Your Device**: Use a USB cable to connect your ESP device to your computer.
4. **Select Firmware**:
   - Click on "Load Firmware".
   - Choose the firmware file you want to flash.
5. **Select Your Port**: In the app, you can choose the connected device port.
6. **Start Flashing**: Click on "Flash" to begin the process. Wait for it to finish, and you will see a success message.
